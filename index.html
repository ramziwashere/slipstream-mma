About John

Certified coaching with a reputation built on trust.

John brings a calm, disciplined, and highly practical approach to every session at Slipstream Martial Arts. He is a certified coach who works alongside the Combat Sports Association, helping students train with structure, safety, and real purpose.

His reputation comes from the way he carries himself: professional, approachable, and committed to getting the best out of every student, whether they are stepping into combat sports for the first time or sharpening serious fight skills.

At Slipstream, John focuses on strong fundamentals, respectful training culture, and steady progress. The goal is simple: help people become fitter, tougher, more confident, and better prepared.
